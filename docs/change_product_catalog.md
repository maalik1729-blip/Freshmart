# Change Record: Product Catalog & Detail Page Iterations

This document tracks the layout and styling updates applied to the product catalog and individual product detail pages.

## Layout Iterations

### 1. Emojis and Placeholders
*   Initially introduced category-specific emojis (e.g. 🥛 for Dairy, 🥤 for Beverages) in the products grid and details layout to serve as visual placeholders before backend upload integration.
*   Replaced the emojis with high-resolution product photos to build a premium mock design catalog.
*   Restored a clean, grey minimalist placeholder box to maintain a sleek, clean appearance for items without active images.

### 2. Transition to Minimalist Text Presentation
*   Refactored the product listing and detail layouts to a text-only card layout to ensure fast page load speeds and clean presentation.
*   Fixed a bug where categories of placeholder items did not align with their catalog tags.
